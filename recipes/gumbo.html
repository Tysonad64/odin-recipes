Gumbo Recipe

The most rich gumbo you have ever tried and how to create this is all right here. Get your flavor on.

We'll take you step by step through making the dish and setting you up with all the ingredients you'll need.

Ingredients

  • Andoullie sausage
  • Chicken Breast
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • Creole seasoning
  • Cajun seasoning
  • Chicken stock
  • Okra
  • Celery
  • Cayenne pepper
  • salt
  • cookingo oil
  • flour

Steps

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Trim and dice the bell pepper, onion, and celery. Mince the garlic and add it all to a bowl. Put all of the seasonings into a small bowl and put off to the side.
  2. Prepare the chicken: Dice the chicken into 1-inch pieces. Season the chicken with new Creole, cajun, salt, and Cayenne pepper.
  3. Cook the sausage: Add the Andoullie sausage slices into a pan on medium heat for about 3-5 minutes or until lightly brown.
  4. Make the roux: reduce heat to medium-low, add 1/4 cup of cooking oiland 1/2 cup flour to a pot. Cook and whisk frequently until the roux reaches a milk chocolate color, about 10-15 minutes. stir constantly to prevent burning.
  5. Saute the aromatics: Stir in the remaining seasonings and garlic, celery, bell peppers, and onions into a pan and cook for about 5-7 minutes.
  6. Build the stew: Gradually whisk in 6 cups of the chicken stock. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium-low.
  7. Add protiens: stir in the sausage and the chicken. continue to cook stirring occasionally for 1-3 hours. If the gumbo becomes too thick and in more reserved chicken stock.
  8. Portion and serve: At this point the gumbo is ready, pick your bowl and ladel in the stew. Can be placed over cooked white rice for a more hearty meal
